WaveNet's plans

At WaveNet, we understand how important our service is to stay connected in this age. We offer many plans to cater to the needs of our customers, from basic broadband plans to specialised NBN.

We are always looking to help you in your journey, tailoring your experience to what you request. Our goal is to be the most helpful and responsive to our customers beyond any other company.


Our Standard Plan is perfect for individuals and small households. It is a broadband plan offering unlimited data, suitable for up to 3 users, with typical evening speeds of 25 Mbps, for only $62.99 per month.

Our SuperWave Plan fits well for families and small businesses. It is an NBN plan with unlimited data, suitable for up to 3 users, has typical evening speeds of 50 Mbps, for only $74.99 per month.

Our UltraWave Plan is great for larger buildings requiring better internet service. It extends on SuperWave, allowing up to 6 users, 90 Mbps typical evening speeds, all for $84.99 per month.
